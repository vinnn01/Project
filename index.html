    Projects

    Midas System Log Checker

    The MIDAS Log Checker is an essential tool at STMicroelectronics, designed to monitor and analyze log files generated by the MIDAS system. It performs regular scans to detect anomalies and errors in real-time, ensuring the stability and reliability of critical processes. By automating log file analysis, it facilitates early detection of issues, allowing swift intervention by operations teams to maintain optimal system performance.

    PTMQD System Log Checker

    The PTMQD Log Checker is a vital component of STMicroelectronics' infrastructure, specializing in monitoring and analyzing log files from the PTMQD system. It operates by continuously parsing log entries to identify potential anomalies or errors promptly. This proactive approach enables operations teams to address issues swiftly, ensuring uninterrupted functionality across PTMQD-related processes and systems.

    Archiving Forecast

    Archiving forecast automation involves predicting future archival dates based on historical patterns or preset schedules. It optimizes storage management, ensuring data availability and compliance with retention policies.
